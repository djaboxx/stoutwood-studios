## The Story

This table is built from wood that had no business becoming something beautiful:
- Furniture pulled free off Facebook Marketplace
- Pallets from Buffalo Billiards
- Plywood scraps from the shop floor

That's not a flaw. That's the whole point.

**Key features:**
- Heavy duty casters (it moves, it works)
- Two large drawers — deep enough for tools, gloves, supplies
- Lower tier shelf sized for bags of potting soil
- Built once. Never built again exactly like this.

**Custom order terms:** ~$700–$800 · ~3 week lead time · Inquire via DM
